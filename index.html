"Discover your Community"

"Discover Your Community" is a Human-Computer Interaction project that I developed with two classmates for my Human-Computer Interaction class, whose theme was reducing disparity. Our aim was to create a space for the closeted LGBTQ+ community to connect and find resources in a safer manner. The project consisted of two parts; in the first, we discussed the problem, research process and possible designs solutions, and in the second, we performed usability and heuristic testing to come up with a final app prototype. Through this project, I demonstrated my understanding of the user-centered design process and my ability to create a solution that addresses a real-world problem.

Rover Swarm Co-Localization

During the summer of 2022, I was part of a student team working on a project for Lunar Outpost Inc. Our objective was to develop a method to localize rovers in a monocular camera frame using computer vision and machine learning techniques. Our team tackled the challenge of determining the transformation matrix that represents the position, orientation, and scale of the rover in the camera frame. Using a combination of feature detection and recognition, linear algebra, machine larning and computer vision techniques, we were able to extract meaningful information from company provided datasets and produce a transformation matrix. Adhering to Agile and Scrum methodologies, our team created a functional prototype that demonstrated the potential of the solution to be used in real-world scenarios with MAPP rovers.

Population, Temperature, Pollution Database

I analyzed U.S. pollution, population growth, and daily temperatures from 2015 to 2019 by creating a relational database. I performed data wrangling and cleaning, modeled the database using ERD, and normalized it to at least fifth normal form. Using Jupyter notebooks and psycopg2, I designed and populated the database on a school provided server. I then worked with my classmates to analyze the resulting database and gained valuable insights into environmental trends. This project showcases my ability to create and manage a database, as well as my skills in data wrangling, cleaning, modeling, normalization, and data analysis.
